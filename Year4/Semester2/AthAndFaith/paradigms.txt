Paradigms

- philosophical of theoretical framework that shapes the way we view the world, how we investigate the world, and what we are able to perceive as real
- everyone views the world through a paradigm

paradigm shifts:
- originally only applied to sciences, they are major changes in the way people view the world
- examples:
	- geocentric -> heliocentric
	- creationism -> evolution
	- writing -> printing press
	- analog -> digital
	- conversion -> consumerism (of religion)

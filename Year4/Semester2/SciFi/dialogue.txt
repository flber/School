Dialogue

- adds to the reader's knowledge
- keeps the plot moving
- reveals something about the speaker
- dramatizes relationships
- adverbs tend to weaken description

Not:
- substitute for direct narrative
- no extended brooding

Tips:
- gesture is part of dialogue
- dialogue is what character do to one another
- silence is part of dialogue
- dialogue is not necessarily grammatically correct
- dialogue does not occur in a vacuum
